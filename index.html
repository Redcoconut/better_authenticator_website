Get Started

How it works

Step 1:

When prompted for an authentication code simply click on the extention and click "request".

Step 2:

You will recieve a notification on your paired phone, just click on it to open the app.

Step 3:

Click on fill and it will automatic fill in the number in your browser and log you in.

Main Features

Secure

Just as secure as any offline authenticator app. The secret codes NEVER leave your device.

Fast

Yup, its just as secure, but you get in much faster than with any other authenticator app.

Free

Thats right, this app is 100% free. No ads, and no selling your data (because there is nothing to sell).

Browser Extention Partner

A broswer extention is available for Chrome Edge and Firefox. The extention already knows which key to request, and if your phone has it. A single click will bring up the app on your phone, the phone doesnt need to be on the app, or even turned on.

Fully Secured

Thats right, ill say it again. There is no way for hackers to get your secret keys, they NEVER leave your device under any circumstances. Only the TOTP tokens are sent to the extention and are encrypted with the Advanced Encryption Standard so that it cannot be read during its journey.

No accounts

Similar to WhatsApp Web, you scan a qr code and are automatically connected. No username, no password, no identifiable information at all.
